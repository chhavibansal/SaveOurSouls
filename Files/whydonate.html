Why are we asking for donation?

We believe in holding the trust of our donars,thereby we put in all the money donated to us for the wellfare of dogs around us. Your continued support is a source of our continous help provided to medically ill dogs. We put in the money to vaccinate dogs,provide food to undernourished stray dogs.Your donation stays with the organisation and helps animals in need.



Thank You for your CONTINUED Support

Where Do We Use Your Funds?

Stray animals are usually reported to be in poor condition by the locality people,who themselves are not able to help the needy animals because of financial problem. We do not blame them, so we have ourselves taken this incentive to render monotory help for ailing dogs and puppies found locally in society.

To what we can thing upto we would ourselves organize vaccination camps for the most essential vaccination needed for dogs like the rabies, DHPP, Distemper, measles, parainfluenza.

We may not be responsible for the plight of these stray animals but we can definetly treat them and help them live a better life. The more we spend on things of charity the more we flourish. It is a humble request from our side that, even a small amount can be of great help.

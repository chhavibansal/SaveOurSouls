About Us

Our mission is to make our community a better place by serving as an advocate for companion animals. Our vision for the future is that no adoptable animal will be euthanized in India, and that animal cruelty and inhumane treatment of animals will cease to exist.

History

A community developed by group of humanitarian to provide a safer environment to the stray animals.Our only purpose was to place abandoned animals into new homes.

We aim to build up small animal hospital, where people walk in voluntarily to provide aid to those in need of immediate help. We wish to grow not just as an organisation but as a community that operates country wide. It is correctyl said that small initiatives turn into a bigger better idea which helps to mold the society thereby making it better place to live bothe for animals and humans.

Welcome to SOS

So what is the AIM of SOS directory? Our mission is to make our community a better place by serving as an advocate for companion animals. We are willing working to build a society wherein no animal is left unattended and a compassionate society is formed. People have joined us from various domains providing help from their end, we are counting on you to join us and make this mission a vision.

So... who are we?

We are a charitable firm who run solely by volunteers. Animals a re housed in foster homes, and the volunteers adopted out the homeless animals without vaccinating, spaying them. The only purpose was to place adandoned animals in new homes. The organization is operational country-wide.

We also occasionally have meetups to spread awarness about our initiative and to reach out to larger masses who are looking for a medium like ours. If, for example, we know we're coming to Delhi, we'll let everyone know and schedule a meet up day to tour the city and take photos. If you'd like us to visit your city let us know!
